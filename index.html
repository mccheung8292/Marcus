Where Artistry Meets Vision

Marcus Cheung is a creative, performer, and innovator who strives to utilize both his creative endeavor and administration skills to impact the arts. He currently perform in a variety of ensembles such as the Miami University Symphony Orchestra and Cello Ensemble. He utilizes his administration skills through his experiences as an orchestra administrator for Festival Prague Summer Nights, Treasurer for Asian American Association and as an Artistic Administration Intern for Summermusik (Cincinnati Chamber Orchestra).

His career goals are to make a positive impact on people by managing and developing ideas and events in the creative arts industry as a performer, innovator, and administrator.
